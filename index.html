Developer, Fashion, Sewing and Embroidery Fiend. Creator and Crafter at heart.

My name is Jasmine and I love to create things. I love colors, if it were up to me colors would be everywhere. If you're wondering; Why should colors be everywhere? Well that's easy. How do you feel when you see a rainbow bursting through the clouds after the rain has stopped? Happy, right? Colors can change anything dull and dreary and give it life. Colors brighten the room, change moods and the atmosphere. Without the colors, the world would be a sad place. I like to create things with bursts of colors.

I have a broad range of skills and experience; from sewing, fashion, accounting to fixing phones and tablets. I wanted to transition into software development to hopefully change the world for the better. Merging Tech with Fashion with would be totally cool! The best part is that, I would be creating something in some form or way.

My favorite resources are Treehouse, Mozilla Developer Network, and occasionally W3Schools. Stack Overflow is also a great community resource for all developers.
